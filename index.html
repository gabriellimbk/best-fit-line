Best-Fit Line Simulator

H2 Chemistry · Data Analysis Practice

  • 1Set Anomalous Point, Scatter Level, and optionally enter a Class Seed — all students using the same 6-digit seed get the identical dataset. Leave blank for a random seed. Click Generate New Data.
  • 2Study the × marks on the graph and judge the overall linear trend — ignore any obvious anomalous data point.
  • 3Click Select / Deselect Anomalous Data Points, then click the × mark to exclude it — draw mode resumes automatically.
  • 4Draw your best-fit straight line — see the gesture guide on this panel for mouse and touch controls.
  • 5Press Submit Answer to see your score, the optimal fit, and detailed feedback.
Key Principle A good best-fit line has roughly equal numbers of × marks above and below it, minimises total deviation, and never connects dot-to-dot. Exclude clearly anomalous points.
